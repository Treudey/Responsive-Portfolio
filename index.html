About Me

I am a passionate programmer out of Toronto looking to make a splash in the Web Development scene. I am attending the UofT Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp and constantly adding to my portfolio.

I got the coding bug only a year ago, but ever since I printed that first “Hello World!”, I have been fully immersing myself in the programming world. Every day I am trying to learn as much as I can about web development and improve my skill set.

I used to be an actor and I bring along my love of interacting with people and enjoy working in an environment with a strong focus on teamwork. I focus on building and maintaining strong relationships with my co-workers in whatever job I work at. I love to make my colleagues and employers happy by working extremely diligently and producing efficient and reliable work.
